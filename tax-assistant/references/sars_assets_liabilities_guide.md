# SARS Statement of Assets and Liabilities Guide

## Local Assets (at Market Value)

### Required Asset Categories

- Fixed Properties and all improvements to properties
- Shares in Private Company or Member's interest in Close Corporation
- Financial Instruments Listed (Shares, unit trusts, etc.) - excluding crypto asset(s)
- Financial instruments - crypto asset(s)
- Business assets
- Trading capital
- Professional practice assets
- Farming assets
- Net working capital
- Cash and bank accounts
- Vehicles
- Jewelry
- Art/collectibles
- Other personal assets
- Any other assets not specifically mentioned

## Foreign Assets and Liabilities

### Applicability

- **This section is applicable to residents only**
- Non-residents do not report foreign assets

### Currency Conversion

- Foreign currency values must be **converted to rand**
- Use exchange rates **as at the end of the year of assessment**
- Reference the forex_rates.csv file for applicable rates

### Required Amounts

#### Foreign Assets

- **Total Foreign Assets at Cost**
- **Total Foreign Assets at Market Value**

#### Foreign Liabilities

- **Total Foreign Liabilities**

### Asset Categories (Same as Local)

- Fixed Properties and improvements
- Private company shares/CC interests
- Listed financial instruments (excluding crypto)
- Crypto assets
- Net capital of business/trade/profession/farming
- Other assets

## Practical Implementation

### Calculation Steps

1. Identify all assets and liabilities individually
2. Determine market values as at year-end
3. Convert foreign currency amounts using year-end rates
4. Calculate totals for each category

## Agent Instructions

### When Processing Financial Data

1. Extract all individual asset and liability items
2. Classify according to SARS categories
3. Use market values as at year-end date
4. Convert foreign currencies using references/forex_rates.csv
5. Calculate totals and verify R50 million threshold
6. Add audit references for each entry
7. Update statement_of_assets_and_liabilities.csv

## For human reference (agents should ignore this)

### Valuation Guidelines

- **Professional valuations are not compulsory**
- SARS accepts market value that is a **reasonable best estimate**
- Definition of reasonable estimate:
  - "An estimate as close as possible to the market value of the particular asset"
  - "An estimate as to which there is a reasonable basis"
  - Must strike a balance fairly and reasonably on available facts
  - Consider all factors that might affect the conclusion
